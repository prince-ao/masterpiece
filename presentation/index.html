Masterpiece

About Masterpiece

Masterpiece is a mobile app for art enthusiasts to explore and discover various artworks while also providing information about their prices. The app aims to bring together artists, galleries, and users in a seamless and visually appealing manner.

Full Stack Creation:
  • Seamlessly combines frontend and backend development for a holistic user experience.
  • Enables end-to-end feature implementation and a cohesive design for Masterpiece.
Monorepo Efficiency:
  • All-in-one code repository housing diverse project components for the app.
  • Simplifies version control, fosters code reusability, and ensures consistent updates.
Microservice Empowerment:
  • Leverages a microservice architecture to split the application into autonomous, specialized units for Masterpiece.
  • Enhances scalability, maintenance, and innovation by independently managing services.
Cloud-Driven Performance:
  • Hosted on cloud infrastructure, ensuring high availability and global accessibility for the app.
  • Harnesses cloud services for data storage, computation, and rapid scaling.

What does Masterpiece do?

Features:

  • Login and Signup: Secure user authentication and registration.
  • Homepage: Central hub for user navigation and app exploration.
  • Profiles: Customizable user profiles to showcase preferences.
  • Upload and Delete Paintings: Artists can manage their artwork.
  • Search for Users: Easy discovery of other users' profiles.
  • Evaluate Price of Paintings: AI-powered price estimation for uploaded images.
  • Buy Paintings: Direct purchase of desired artwork within the app.

Closing Statement

Masterpiece aims to provide art lovers with an immersive and informative experience by enabling them to view art and art prices. By incorporating features such as a comprehensive catalog, personalized collections, and social interaction, the app seeks to engage users and foster a vibrant art community.
